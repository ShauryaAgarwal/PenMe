Why Choose PenMe?

PenMe brings back the personal touch to digital communication. Say goodbye to impersonal messages and hello to handwritten emotions. With our app, you can effortlessly send heartfelt handwritten messages, letters, and greeting cards to your loved ones, while enjoying the convenience of digital technology.

  • Personal Touch: Experience the emotional connection of handwritten messages and cards in the digital era.
  • Easy Handwriting: Write effortlessly using a digital pen or your fingertips, thanks to our patented technology.
  • Multilingual Support: No more keyboard switching for different languages; PenMe adjusts to any device type/size and supports writing in multiple languages seamlessly.
  • Group Collaboration: Collaborate with globally distributed teams to send handwritten group greeting cards or exchange personalized messages.
  • Integrated Payments: Conveniently send money along with your handwritten messages, letters, or greeting cards using our integrated payment modules.
