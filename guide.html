Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Download and Install PenMe

    Visit the App Store or Google Play Store and search for PenMe. Download and install the app on your device.

  2. Sign Up or Log In

    Launch the PenMe app and sign up for a new account or log in with your existing credentials.

  3. Explore the User Interface

    Take a tour of PenMe's intuitive and user-friendly interface. Familiarize yourself with the different features and options available.

  4. Create a New Handwritten Message

    Tap on the "New Message" button to start creating a handwritten message. Use your digital pen or fingertips to write your heartfelt message on the screen.

  5. Personalize Your Message

    Customize your handwritten message by selecting different colors, pen styles, and writing effects. Add stickers, emojis, or personal drawings to make your message truly unique.

  6. Send Your Handwritten Message

    Once you're satisfied with your handwritten message, choose the recipient and send it digitally. PenMe ensures your message retains the personal touch, even in the digital world.
